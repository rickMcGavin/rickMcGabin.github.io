About Me

Hi, I'm Rick. I'm a call center operations manager, and I'm also a Web Developer. I've spent the past 4 years working in customer service for health insurance. I've advanced quickly from customer service representative to customer care supervisor to call center operations manager in my first two years with current company.

For the past year, I've spent my evenings teaching myself web development. For the first 3 months I learned using sites like Codecademy and Treehouse to learn the basics of HTML, CSS, php, JavaScript, Python, Ruby and Objective-C. After grounding myself in the fundamentals of many languages, I decided to take a more focused approach. I wanted to learn Full Stack Web Development. I'm working through Colt Steele's Web Dev Bootcamp on udemy while also working through the challenges on freecodecamp.

My ideal projects and jobs would combine my passions for customer service and technology. Due to my background in call center management, I'm heavily interested in ways technology can be used to provide simple and efficient solutions for customer service representatives.
